Repository storage
------------------

- asv_counts.compact.b64.part001
- asv_counts.compact.b64.part002
- asv_counts.compact.b64.part003
- asv_counts.compact.b64.part004

R/00_restore_abundance.R reconstructs data/asv_abundance.tsv automatically.
